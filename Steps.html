Steps

1. What key information do you need to know?

  • Credit Hours - The number of hours assigned to a course, usually between 1 to 5 hours, based on the course's workload and complexity.

  • Grade Value - The numeric value assigned to a letter grade (e.g., A = 4.0, B = 3.0, C = 2.0, D = 1.0, F = 0.0).

  • Grade Points - The result of multiplying Credit Hours by the Grade Value for a course (e.g., a 3-credit course with an A (4.0) yields 12 grade points).

  • Attempted Hours - The total number of credit hours you have enrolled in, regardless of whether you passed or failed the courses.

  • Earned Hours - The total number of credit hours you have successfully completed by passing the courses.

  • Term GPA - The GPA calculated for a specific semester or term, considering only the courses taken during that time.

  • Cumulative GPA - The overall GPA calculated using all the grades and credit hours from all completed terms up to the present.

2. Basic Formula

  • What is the formula for calculating GPA for one semester?
    • Step One: Calculate the grade point based on the semester.
      • For each class Convert the letter grade to credit value.

        • EX: Getting a A in a certain class means the Letter grade is 4.0.

      • After that multiply the Grade values by the corolating credit hours to get the grade points for each class.

        • $$GradePoint = Credit Hour X Grade Value$$

      • At the end add all the grade points and then divid them by the total credit hours.

        • $$TotalGradePoints = Grade points + GradePoints....$$

    • Step Two: Calculate the total amount of Credit Hours
      • Add all the Credit hours in the semester.

        • $$TotalCreditHours = Credit Hours + Credit Hours... $$

    • Step Three: Now divide the TotalGradePoints by the TotalCreditHours

      • $$GPA = \frac{TotalGradePoints}{TotalCreditHours} $$

  • What is the formula for calculating the cumilative GPA?
    • So for the cumilative GPA follow steps 1 and 2 after that add all the total:

        • $$GPA = \frac{SemesterOneGPA + SemesterTwoGPA...}{SemesterOneCreditHours + SemesterTwoCreditHours...}$$
